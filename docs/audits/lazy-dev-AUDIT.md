It cannot be both dead cruft and the live governing roadmap. "No code imports it" is true, but `REALIGNMENT/` is **living design documentation**, not dead code. Treat it as **"archive or keep — your call,"** not auto-safe-delete. With `REALIGNMENT/` reclassified, the *real* zero-risk Tier-1 code/data cut drops to **~1.3k LOC** (sql, docker, stray scripts, stale manifest lines); the big LOC wins all live in Tier 2 behind small untangles.

**Net honest picture:** of ~91k LOC, the scout's two largest hoped-for cuts (telemetry + wiki ≈ 10k) are **not** cuttable. Genuinely actionable: **~1.3k LOC zero-risk now**, **~9.3k LOC after small export/import untangles**, **~35 lines of internal shrink**. The "amputation" is incomplete and the leftover modules are more entangled with the keep-set than the handoff claims.
